# Labor Analgesia


## CSE Spinal

- **0-4cm)** Fent 10-25mcg
- **4-8cm)** F10-25mcg **+ 0.5cc Bupi 0.25%**
- **8-10cm)** F10-25mcg **+ 1.0cc Bupi 0.25%**
